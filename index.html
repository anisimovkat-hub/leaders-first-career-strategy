FAQ

Before you book

Practical answers about the format, preparation and what is — and is not — included.

01Will you rewrite my CV or LinkedIn profile?

These are strategy sessions, not generic done-for-you writing services. I show you what is not working, how to position your experience and what to change first. CV rewriting is not included; full LinkedIn rewriting can be discussed as a separate package.

02Do I need to prepare?

Yes. After booking, you will complete a focused questionnaire and, depending on the session, share your LinkedIn profile, target roles or example vacancies. This lets us spend the session on decisions and strategy — not basic information gathering.

03Can you help with an international move?

Yes. I work with international professionals considering the UK, Italy, Switzerland and wider European markets. We can assess realistic role options, positioning, visibility and the steps to take before applying.

04Is this suitable for senior professionals?

Yes. It is especially useful when you have strong experience but need a clearer story, stronger market positioning or a realistic route into a more senior role.

05What happens after I book?

You choose a time, pay online and receive the pre-session questionnaire. I review your answers before we meet, so our time stays focused on clarity, positioning and practical next steps.
